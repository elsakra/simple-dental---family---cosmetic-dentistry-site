# Simple Dental - Site Routes

This document lists all the routes created for the Simple Dental website and their corresponding purposes.

## Generated Routes

### Main Pages
- `/` - Homepage (src/pages/index.astro)
  - Main landing page with hero, services overview, about section, testimonials, and contact form
  - Corresponds to original site root

- `/about` - About Us (src/pages/about.astro)
  - Detailed information about the practice, team, mission, and values
  - Includes team member profiles and practice history

- `/services` - Services (src/pages/services.astro)
  - Comprehensive list of all dental services offered
  - Detailed descriptions of general, cosmetic, restorative, pediatric, and emergency dentistry
  - Insurance and payment information

- `/team` - Our Team (src/pages/team.astro)
  - Detailed profiles of dentists and staff members
  - Team credentials, specialties, and experience
  - Team values and philosophy

- `/contact` - Contact Us (src/pages/contact.astro)
  - Contact form for appointment scheduling
  - Office information, hours, and location details
  - New patient information and directions

## Route Structure

All routes follow a clean, SEO-friendly URL structure:
- No trailing slashes
- Descriptive, keyword-rich paths
- Consistent with dental practice standards

## Navigation Integration

All routes are properly integrated into:
- Header navigation menu
- Footer links
- Internal cross-linking throughout the site
- Mobile navigation menu

## Content Mapping

Since the original site was behind a security check, content was created based on:
- Standard dental practice services and information
- Best practices for dental website content
- SEO-optimized structure and messaging
- Local Katy, TX market focus

## Technical Implementation

- All routes use Astro's static site generation
- Consistent layout structure with Layout.astro
- Responsive design across all pages
- Proper meta tags and SEO optimization
- Structured data implementation
